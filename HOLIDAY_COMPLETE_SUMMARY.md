## 🎯 How to Use

### Step 1: Access the Tab
- Open your dashboard
- Look for the **"Holidays"** or **"HD"** button (2nd from left)

### Step 2: View Holidays
- See all 6 holidays in a beautiful card layout
- Each card shows:
  - Holiday name
  - Start and end dates
  - Duration (1 day or multi-day)
  - Description

### Step 3: Check Statistics
- Total holidays count
- Multi-day holidays count
- Upcoming holidays information

### Step 4: Read Parent Tips
- Plan activities ahead
- Keep a routine
- Balance rest and activity
- Communicate with school
